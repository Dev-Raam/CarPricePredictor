Features

Our Car Price Predictor uses advanced machine learning algorithms to give you a near-accurate price estimate for your used car. You can also rotate and view a 3D car model, compare currency rates, and search for popular models in our database.

About

This project aims to provide a user-friendly platform for car enthusiasts and potential buyers to get quick, reliable price estimates for used vehicles. Developed using HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Python (Flask), and Three.js for the 3D model.
